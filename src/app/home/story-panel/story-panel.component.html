STORY TELLING OF MINO HOTEL

Nestled in the serene and leafy neighborhood of Casablanca, just a short 5-minute drive from the bustling business center, the enchanting Corniche, and the prestigious Royal Golf Course, Mino Hotel embodies the essence of this captivating destination. Inspired by the timeless Art Deco era, our hotel boasts a minimalist architectural design that exudes sophistication. Surrounded by a lush garden oasis in the heart of the city, Mino Hotel proudly showcases its distinctive charm as an Urban Resort, offering an atmosphere of cozy elegance that is simply unparalleled.
